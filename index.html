What Is Braces Treatment?

Braces treatment is a specialized orthodontic procedure designed to correct dental alignment and bite issues. It involves the use of brackets, wires, and other appliances to gradually shift your teeth into their ideal positions. Whether you’re dealing with crowding, spacing, or an overbite, braces can help improve not just the look of your teeth but also their function and long-term health.

Benefits of Getting Braces

  • Improved Oral Health: Straight teeth are easier to clean, reducing the risk of cavities and gum disease.
  • Enhanced Confidence: A straight, well-aligned smile can significantly boost self-esteem and overall appearance.
  • Better Bite Alignment: Properly aligned teeth prevent jaw strain and uneven wear on teeth.
  • Long-Term Results: With proper care and maintenance, the results from braces treatment can last a lifetime.

Types of Braces Treatment

At Sabinsky Orthodontics, patients can choose from several options based on their preferences, lifestyle, and orthodontic needs. Popular types include:

  • Traditional Metal Braces: The most common and effective option for correcting complex dental issues.
  • Ceramic Braces: Designed to blend in with your natural teeth, offering a more aesthetic look.
  • Clear Aligners: A removable and discreet alternative for those who want a nearly invisible treatment.
  • Lingual Braces: Placed behind the teeth, offering complete invisibility during treatment.
